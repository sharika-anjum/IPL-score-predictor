In our cricket crazy country, cricket fans have lauded the IPL. Over the years, IPL has become the most watched tournament.

It became a platform for the emerging Indian cricketers.

We all are so busy hyping about our team that we fail to hold that craze and go for predicting which team will win in which match.

And here's lie the sole reason for this web app to come up. A neural network based approach which will predict a team's score based on certain criteria.
